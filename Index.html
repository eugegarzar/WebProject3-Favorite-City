Velaris, City of Starlight

Night Court, Prythian (Not on Earth!)

Welcome to Velaris

Velaris is a breathtaking city hidden in the mountains of the Night Court. Known as the City of Starlight, it is protected from darkness and war by powerful enchantments. With its serene river, artistic districts, and celestial skies, Velaris is a haven for dreamers and creatives. Unfortunately for me, this is a fictional city which I can only visit in my dreams. The city of Velaris is featured in the book series A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J. Mass. If you haven't already, please read this book series! If you love a good fantasy/romance book, you will love ACOTAR.

Starfall Festival

Starfall is one of Velaris’ most beloved events. Each year, spirits from another realm travel across the sky like falling stars, creating a surreal experience. Locals gather to celebrate the magic and beauty of life. Learn more.

Top Places to Visit

Rainbow of Velaris: This vibrant district overflows with galleries, music halls, and artisan studios. Artists and performers come here to share their creativity. Read more.

House of Wind: Sitting high above Velaris, this architectural marvel is home to warriors, scholars, and breathtaking views. Although you can't enter unless invited, it is still worth it to see it even from afar. Learn more.

Sidra River: The heart of the city, the Sidra offers peaceful walks, scenic views, and moonlit serenity. Explore more.

Interesting Facts About Velaris

  • Location: Northern part of the Night Court, hidden by magic.
  • Weather: Mild climate with star-filled nights.
  • Language: Common tongue of Prythian, with Old Fae influences.
  • Special Protection: Shielded by Rhysand’s magic for centuries. Can't be seen by enemies.
